About The Joon Foundation

Together, we can make a difference to the lives of children, teens and helpless individuals across this diverse and vibrant region. Your support can bring hope, and a brighter future to countless children.

The Joon Foundation boldly invests in creative solutions to urgent challenges, sparking hope for our future.

Our mission is to support and uplift marginalized individuals and families, both in South Korea and globally, who have suffered from poverty, natural disasters, child abuse, military abuse, and exploitation by record labels. Through compassion, advocacy, and direct assistance, we aim to provide essential services, financial relief, and opportunities for empowerment. We believe in equality, justice, and the power of community. Our ultimate goal is to create sustainable change for those in need, ensuring they have the resources, support, and opportunities to build brighter futures.
